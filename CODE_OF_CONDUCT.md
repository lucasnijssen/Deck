# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want Deck to be a respectful, constructive, and technically serious open-source project.

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful in language and tone
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person
- Assume good intent, while still being clear and direct
- Welcome new contributors and help them get oriented
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or intimidation
- Insults, slurs, or discriminatory language
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or reviews
- Publishing private information without consent
- Repeated bad-faith behavior after being asked to stop

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private security report or contact the maintainers through the repository’s security process.
